About Us

Saint Nishchal Sr. Sec. School

The school is affiliated to CBSE New Delhi up to Sr. Sec. Classes having all streams I.e.Scienxe,Commerce and humanities.  In play group students are taught by play way methods.Stress free environment is provided by using toys and audiovisual aids.

The curriculum of primary Classes is a combination of academics and curricular activities for overall growth of students.  Middle wing consists of Classes VI to VIII. Sanskrit is taught as third language in middle section.

Sr. Sec. Section of school consists of classes IX to XII. It offers an approach to education where each student's performance is evaluated personally.
