About Us

Welcome to Saint Nishchal Sr Sec School

Saint Nishchal Sr Sec School in Hisar, Haryana, offers a well-rounded education that nurtures academic, social, and personal growth. With a focus on excellence, we prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow through dedicated teaching, modern facilities, and a supportive environment

Our dedicated team of educators fosters a stimulating environment where students are encouraged to excel in academics, sports, and co-curricular activities.
